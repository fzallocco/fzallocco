Welcome to my page!

I am a graduate Computer Science student at Pace University completing my Master's program in May 2024, and I am seeking opportunities to make a meaningful impact. Currently, I am supporting the Black Boys Leadership Alliance as a Junior Application Developer (JAD), producing software to solve social issues in the NYC black and brown community.

My journey in technology started all the way back in 2020 during my senior year in college. Courses like Intro to Python, Web Design, and Cybersecurity, gave me a sufficient basis to understand most technolgies. Shortly after graduation, I accepted a role as a Business Development Representative with Micro Focus (now OpenText). During my tenure with the company, I rigorously studied the company's product suite and developed rapport-building skills with c-suite managers across government, healthcare, & banking. I then realized that my true passion was understanding how software works and the advancements it brings to society. Thus, I decided to leave the company and pursue a Master's degree in software engineering in the US, leading me to the Seidenberg School in New York City.
